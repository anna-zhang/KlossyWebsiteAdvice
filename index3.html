Meet Our Founder, Karlie Kloss

The supermodel first became interested in coding by wondering how her favorite apps like Instagram worked. This prompted her to sign up for a class at the Flatiron School where she learned the basics of web applications and how to write code in languages like Ruby on Rails. Karlie says the goal of the program is to introduce computer programming in a fun and collaborative way, and to "change the stigma of 'this is just for boys'."
